About us

The iPhone is a line of smartphones developed by Apple Inc.,
first introduced in 2007. It combines mobile telephone, digital camera, music player,
and personal computing technologies, running on Apple's iOS operating system.
The iPhone has revolutionized the smartphone industry with its innovative design and features,
allowing users to make calls, send texts, and access the internet via cellular networks or Wi-Fi.
Over the years, various models have been released, each enhancing the user experience and integrating
seamlessly within the Apple ecosystem.
